When to Use This Calculator

  • When you suspect a PIP / restructure / managed-out pattern but it's still deniable
  • When your manager's tone has shifted and you want to separate noise vs signal
  • Before you escalate, raise a grievance, or involve HR — so you do it from a position of strength
  • When you need to decide: stabilise, document, escalate, or exit
  • When you want a risk view that includes politics, process, and personal bandwidth

Pro Tips

  • Rate observable patterns, not your worst-case imagination
  • High risk doesn't mean panic — it means tighten evidence + options
  • Look for clusters: one spike is manageable; 3–4 spikes is a forecast
  • Re-run after any: org change, missed deadline, HR meeting, or new manager
  • Use the output as your private plan — not something to "prove" to other people
