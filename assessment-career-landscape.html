CAREER LANDSCAPE ASSESSMENT

Know the real terrain: leverage, risk, options, and your next best move

This assessment helps you separate feelings from facts — and build a plan that makes sense in the UK workplace: internal politics, pay bands, job market friction, redundancy risk, and what actually moves decisions.

When to Use This

  • You feel “stuck”, underpaid, or under-recognised and need a grounded route out.
  • You suspect you’re being managed out, reorganised, or quietly sidelined.
  • You’re considering a move, but you don’t trust your read of the market.
  • You want a raise or promotion and need leverage, not hope.
  • You need clarity on whether to stabilise, push internally, or exit strategically.

What You’ll Get

  • A clear read on your internal leverage and external leverage.
  • A realistic risk signal (not paranoia — indicators).
  • A set of recommended moves based on your scores.
  • Scripts for conversations that sound professional (not emotional or entitled).
  • A 30-day plan to improve your position, whichever direction you choose.
