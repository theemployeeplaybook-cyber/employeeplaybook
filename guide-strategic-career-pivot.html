When to Use This Guide

  • When you feel your role has plateaued and you want a strategic next move.
  • When you’re tempted to resign but you’re not sure you’ve reduced risk first.
  • When internal politics, restructure fog, or shifting priorities are affecting your prospects.
  • When you want to translate achievements into business impact stories.
  • When you need language for growth conversations without sounding entitled.
  • When you’re deciding between a stay plan, internal move, or external pivot.

Pro Tips

  • Don’t confuse frustration with strategy: pivots work when you’re moving towards a clear value story.
  • Reduce risk before you “go loud”: build evidence, references, and a plan to protect momentum.
  • Internal moves are often faster than external moves — if you can align to priorities and sponsors.
  • In the UK, keep things professional and proportionate — build a narrative, not a grievance.
  • If you’re near a restructure, understand your leverage and timing before you trigger anything.
