When to Use This

  • When you work hard but your name doesn't come up when promotions or top projects are discussed.
  • When you want to grow income, influence, or options without burning more hours.
  • When feedback feels vague ("be more strategic") and you want something you can act on.
  • When you're changing roles or recovering from a reputational wobble and need a clean narrative.
  • When you want a 14-day proof plan that creates undeniable signal.

Pro Tips

  • Rate based on what you can do under pressure, not on a good day.
  • Impact is not effort — it's change: decisions made, risk reduced, time saved, revenue protected.
  • "Visibility" isn't performative. It's traceability: can others cite your decisions and outcomes?
  • Focus your upgrades on the dimensions that unlock leverage: clarity, influence, prioritisation, and proof.
  • Use the skills sidebar for "micro-moves" that raise signal without inviting politics.
