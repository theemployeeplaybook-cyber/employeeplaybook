PRESENCE CALIBRATION ASSESSMENT

Calibrate your response when the stakes rise

Presence isn’t your personality. It’s the sum of how you communicate, hold your space, and signal authority under pressure.
Explore Categories

When to Use This Assessment

  • Before a high-stakes meeting, interview, or performance conversation
  • When you feel “competent” but not taken as seriously as you should be
  • When you’re being interrupted, second-guessed, or talked over more than usual
  • When pressure makes you rush, over-explain, or lose your centre
  • When you want a practical baseline to track presence improvements over time

Pro Tips

  • Rate your default under pressure, not your best day
  • Look for clusters: the pattern matters more than a single low score
  • Low scores aren’t “bad” — they’re precise targets for upgrades
  • Re-run monthly, or after any major role/manager change
  • Use the summary as a private plan — not something you need to “prove” to anyone
