BURNOUT PREVENTION PLAN

Protect capacity, credibility, and delivery — before the strain forces a reset you did not choose

This is a prevention plan designed for high-output roles, messy realities, and workplaces that quietly normalise overload. Use it to identify early capacity erosion, separate structural load from behavioural over-functioning, and lock clear expectations while you still have leverage.

When to Use This Plan

  • When you are still delivering, but the cost is rising every week.
  • When your “normal” has become constant catch-up, late nights, and a shrinking recovery window.
  • When you are absorbing ambiguity (unclear priorities, politics, shifting definitions of “urgent”).
  • When you are carrying hidden responsibility (fixer, buffer, translator, peacemaker, sponge).
  • When you want to intervene early — while you still have energy to negotiate change.

How to Use It (fast)

  • Write bluntly. Accuracy beats presentation — this is decision prep.
  • Separate what is structural (work design, resourcing, expectations) from what is behavioural (over-functioning, people-pleasing, avoidance).
  • Choose prevention moves that are visible enough to reshape expectations — not just private self-care.
  • Use scripts to lock trade-offs in writing. Unwritten trade-offs do not exist.
  • The objective is sustainable delivery and reputation protection, at the same time.
