PRESENCE 90 SECOND RESET

Reset your signal in 90 seconds

This is for moments you’re about to talk, reply, or react — and you can feel your signal slipping (rushing, over-explaining, softness, emotional heat). 90 seconds buys you control.

When to Use the 90s Reset

  • Right before you speak in a tense or political moment
  • When you feel yourself rushing, softening, or over-explaining
  • When you’re about to send a message you might regret
  • After being interrupted or undermined and you need to re-enter cleanly
  • When your body is “hot” but your thinking needs to be sharp

Pro Tips

  • Don’t add more words — add more control
  • Slow pace = authority. Fast pace = threat signal
  • Keep your message to one ask, one outcome, one next step
  • If you can’t do all 90 seconds, do Phase 1 only (20s)
  • Write a one-line anchor before you hit send
