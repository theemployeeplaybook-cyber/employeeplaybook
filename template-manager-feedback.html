Manager Feedback Template

Move from generic praise to specific, actionable feedback: situations, behaviors, impact, and development actions. Build confidence, address gaps, and create clear growth pathways for your team members.

When to Use This Template

  • After observing performance that needs specific, actionable feedback.
  • During regular 1:1s to structure developmental conversations.
  • When preparing for performance reviews or appraisals.
  • When addressing behavioral issues or capability gaps.
  • Any time you want to give feedback that builds capability rather than just criticises.

How to Use It (fast)

  • Describe the specific situation (when, where, what happened).
  • Identify the behaviors you observed (actions, words, approaches).
  • Explain the impact (on team, results, relationships, outcomes).
  • Agree development actions (specific, measurable, time-bound).
  • Follow up: "Let's check in on [date] to see how the changes are working."
