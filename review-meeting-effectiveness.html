MEETING EFFECTIVENESS REVIEW

Turn meetings from "time spent" into decisions, clarity, and momentum

This tool helps you review a meeting with precision: what it was meant to achieve, what actually happened, why it drifted, and what to change next time. You'll get an effectiveness scorecard, a "fix plan" (before / during / after), and ready-to-send follow-up scripts that lock outcomes without creating friction.

When to Use This

  • After a meeting that felt draining, unclear, or "busy" without decisions.
  • When meetings keep recurring but outcomes don't change.
  • When you need a calm way to request agenda, time-boxing, or decision ownership.
  • When status updates are swallowing deep work.
  • Before you escalate "meeting load" as a capacity risk.
Most meetings fail on design, not effort.

How to Use It (fast)

  • Score the meeting on 5 dimensions (purpose, decisions, participation, time use, follow-through).
  • Tick the dysfunctions you observed (no blame—just pattern recognition).
  • Write 2–5 lines on what would have made it "worth it".
  • Generate your pack: scorecard, fixes, and scripts.
  • Use the follow-up email template to lock outcomes and prevent drift.
