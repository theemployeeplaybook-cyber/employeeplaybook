When to Use This Map

  • When you feel flooded and can’t separate urgency from importance
  • When you’re “coping” but your decision quality is slipping
  • When you suspect risk is building (HR, reputation, conflict, burnout) but it’s blurred
  • When you need a 72-hour stabilisation sequence, not another framework
  • When you need to communicate clearly without emotional leakage

Pro Tips

  • Rate now, not your “best day” or potential
  • High scores are not “bad” — they are signal
  • Look for clusters: pressure often concentrates into 2–3 root drivers
  • Add a note where the number is high — that’s where your first move sits
  • Use the 72-hour plan to reduce pressure before you try to “perform through it”
